Smart Borrowing Tips

Protect yourself. Build your credit. Save money.

🏆
Try Hustler Fund first — alwaysAt 8% p.a. it is Kenya's cheapest loan. Far cheaper than any mobile app or bank. Dial *254# to check your limit instantly.
📊
SACCOs beat everyoneSACCO rates (12% p.a.) are lower than banks and mobile apps. If your employer has a SACCO, join immediately and start saving.
💡
Timiza is a hidden gemAbsa Timiza charges only 1.083% per month — much cheaper than Tala or Branch. Up to KSh 150,000. Dial *848# or download the app.
🆓
Zenka first loan is FREENew Zenka users get 0% interest on their very first loan. Use this to build your credit profile at zero cost.
📈
Grow your mobile loan limitRepay early. Save on the app. Transact on M-Pesa daily. Every on-time repayment grows your limit automatically over time.
⚠️
Beware Fuliza and ZashFuliza charges 1% daily (365%+ p.a.). Zash charges 2.08% daily. Only use for same-day emergencies. Repay within 24 hours.
🔍
Check your CRB status nowDial *433# (Metropol) or *515# (TransUnion) — both are free. Being listed blocks bank loans and some apps. Clear debts to be removed.
🏦
Best banks for individualsKCB, Equity, and Co-op Bank are most accessible at ~13–14% p.a. Bring your payslip, national ID and 3–6 months bank statement.
🆘
Struggling to repay?Contact your lender BEFORE the due date. Tala, Branch and Zenka offer extensions. Ignoring a loan leads to CRB listing and penalty fees.
🤝
Women-only optionsLadies — Fanisi Credit and KWFT offer loans exclusively for women at very competitive rates. Fanisi first loan: KSh 20,000–30,000.
