Smart Borrowing Tips

Borrow wisely. Build your credit.

🏆
Always try Hustler Fund first At only 8% per year, it's the cheapest loan in Kenya — far cheaper than any other app. Dial *254# to check your limit.
📈
Increase your loan limit Repay early, save regularly on the app, and keep your M-Pesa active. Each on-time repayment grows your limit.
⚠️
Beware of Fuliza and M-Shwari Fuliza charges 1% daily — that's ~365% per year! Only use it for very short emergencies. Always repay the same day if possible.
🔍
Check your CRB status Dial *433# (Metropol) to check if you're listed. Being listed blocks most loan apps. Clear old debts first.
💳
Build your credit profile Use M-Pesa daily. Pay bills via M-Pesa. Buy airtime. Receive income on M-Pesa. Higher activity = higher loan limits.
🆘
Struggling to repay? Contact Tala or Branch BEFORE the due date. Many apps offer extensions. Ignoring a loan leads to CRB listing which blocks future borrowing.
💡
Timiza is hidden gold Absa Timiza charges only 1.083% per month — much lower than Tala or Branch. Great for amounts up to KSh 150,000. Download from Play Store.
